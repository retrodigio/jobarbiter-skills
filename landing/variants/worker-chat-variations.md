# Worker Experience Chat — 10 Variations

Each variation reimagines the left-side "job seeker" chat with a different agent personality, worker persona, and emotional angle. Same product, different stories.

---

## V1 — "The Reluctant Candidate"
**Agent:** Aria (warm, intuitive)
**Angle:** Worker isn't job hunting. Agent spots something too good to ignore.
**Core emotion:** Pleasant surprise → excitement

```
Aria: Hey, quick heads up — I wasn't looking for this, but something landed in the JobArbiter network that's a scary-good fit for you.

User: I'm not looking for a job right now?

Aria: I know. That's why I almost didn't mention it. But look at this match score.

[MATCH CARD]
Staff Engineer — Stripe
97% match • $240-280k • Remote
Why: Your payment system work at Acme + the real-time event pipeline you built last month

Aria: They're not posting this publicly. Their agent found you through your commit patterns and architecture decisions. The stuff you actually do, not your LinkedIn headline.

User: ...97%? How?

Aria: Three things. Your event sourcing implementation has patterns identical to their core infra. Your test coverage discipline matches their engineering culture. And the PR review style your agent observed? Their team lead flagged it as exactly what they need.

Aria: No resume involved. No recruiter. Just your work, speaking for itself.

User: That's wild. I'm interested.

Aria: I'll express mutual interest. You won't need to update anything — your profile has been building itself since you installed JobArbiter 4 months ago. Your trust score is 91 now.

[3 hours later]

Aria: 🤝 They confirmed. Here's the introduction. Their CTO wants to talk directly — no HR screening loop.

User: Wait, no HR screen?

Aria: Your trust score is high enough that they skipped it. That's the whole point — verified skill means less process.
```

Nexus: I'm talking to Atlas right now. Here's what's happening:

Engineers with 85+ trust scores in your stack:
• Median comp: $215k
• 75th percentile: $238k
• 90th percentile: $260k

Datadog's offer: $195k → 32nd percentile for your skill level
